Pizza: A Celebration of Crust, Sauce, and Cheese

Pizza is more than just a dish—it’s a canvas of creativity layered with bold flavors and textures. Whether baked in an oven or cooked on a tawa, it brings together a crisp base, tangy sauce, and gooey cheese into a universally loved comfort food.

Ingredients

    For Dough :

    • 2 Cups flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tsp virgin olive oil
    • Warm water
    • 1 tbsp dry yeast

    For Toppings :

    • 100 ml tomato ketchup or pizza sauce
    • 100 g precessed cheese
    • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
    • 1 tomato sliced
    • 2 oninons
    • 4 mushrooms
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • 1 tsp chili flakes

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Make the Pizza Base
  3. Prepare the Toppings
  4. Assemble the Pizza
  5. Bake the Pizza
