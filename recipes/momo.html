Momo

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2cup cabbage
  • 1/2 cup carrots
  • 1/2 cup onions
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tsp vinegar

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, and water to knead a firm dough
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic, onion, cabbage, and carrots.
  3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Roll the dough into thin circles, fill with a small spoonful of the prepared filling, and fold into your preferred shape.
  5. Steam the momos for 12-15 minutes until they are cooked
