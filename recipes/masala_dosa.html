Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 4-5 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • Oil (for cooking)
  • Salt (to taste)

Cooking Steps

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds overnight. Blend to a smooth paste and ferment for 8-10 hours.
  2. Add salt to the fermented batter and mix well.
  3. Heat oil, splutter mustard seeds, and add curry leaves.
  4. Sauté onions and green chilies, then add turmeric, salt, and mashed potatoes.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan, spread a ladle of batter in a circular motion, and drizzle oil around the edges
  6. Once crisp, place the potato filling in the center, fold the dosa, and serve hot.
