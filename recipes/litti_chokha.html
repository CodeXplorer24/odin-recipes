Litti Chokha

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup sattu
  • 1 tsp ajwain
  • 1rso mustard oil
  • Salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, ajwain, and mustard oil. Knead it into a soft dough using water
  2. mix sattu with mustard oil, salt, ajwain, and water
  3. Take a small portion of the dough, stuff it with the sattu mix, and shape it into balls.
  4. Bake or roast the litti in an oven or over charcoal until golden and cooked.
