Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is one of the popular dish in India. It is the main source of protein for vegetarians. It is mostly consumed with breads or rice.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 cups Palak leaves
  • 200-250 grams Paneer
  • Different Spices
  • 1/2 Cup salt
  • Refined Oil
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Water as needed

Cooking Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add the spinach leaves and cook for 2-3 minutes
  2. Drain and immediately transfer the spinach to a bowl of ice water
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds
  4. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté until fragrant.
  5. Toss in the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  6. Add the spinach puree and a little water to adjust consistency. Let it simmer for 4-5 minutes.
  7. Add the paneer cubes to the spinach gravy and mix gently. Let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes
  8. Stir in garam masala and cream/yogurt (if using) for extra richness
  9. Serve hot with roti, naan, or steamed rice. Enjoy!
