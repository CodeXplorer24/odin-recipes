Veg Biryani

Veg biryani is a regal dish that layers marinated Soyabean and fragrant basmati rice into a harmonious blend of flavor, texture, and aroma. Originating from the royal kitchens, it’s a celebration of culinary artistry—where every grain of rice is infused with spices, and every bite carries the richness of slow-cooked Soyabean.

Ingredients

  • Soyabean - 500g
  • Basmati Rice - 2 cups, soaked 30 minutes
  • Yogurt - 3 tbsp
  • Spices - Garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, bay leaf cardamom,cloves,cinnamon, jeera
  • Onions - 1 large and thin sliced
  • Herbs - green chillies, mint leaves
  • Ghee or Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Water - 3 cups
  • Optional - Fried onions

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Marinate the Soyabean
  2. Prepare Rice
  3. Layer and Dum Cook
